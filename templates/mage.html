this is a map generator






why?


this whole website has deep roots in my passion for discovering the works behind things that seem simple or that we take for granted. it`s the only worthwhile thing i`ve ever done.

i wish to make this as organic as possible, without the knicks and knacks of modern, highly marketed "apps" that lurk the vastness of the internet. that`s why it`s called "ego": it`s me.

trivia: during the production process, i kept calling this "project ego" and i was even thinking of making it the official name of the website. at one point i even searched online to see wether i risked receiving a copyright infringement and i must admit that i had a pleasant surprise: it seems that "project ego" was also the initial name of fable, one of my favourite videogames, while it was in development more than fifteen years ago. nice.




how?


it`s just simple html and python code glued together by the flask framework. there`s also some bootstrap functionality and a little css customisation.




what for?


this is my portfolio; my legacy, so to speak. it`s a catalog of achievements, the results of my efforts. this is all made from passion, not for monetary gain. i`ve always believed that the things you do for money stop being pleasant.

therefore, i invite you to explore this website and come back to it, as it will grow. who knows, maybe one day i will actually finish passers by the wells and you`ll be able to play it.

thank you for your time.
